§ 01 · The product

It's a recording booth
that doesn't need a person.

Standby Booth runs entirely on the iPad. No backend. No cloud. No setup beyond plugging in.

The host configures the event once — branding, prompts, recording length, storage destination — then locks the device into a kiosk and walks away.

The guest tap-records-reviews. Files save to Photos, a local folder, a USB-C drive, or share over AirDrop and QR. The host comes back to a folder full of clips.
